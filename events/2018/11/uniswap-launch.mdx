Built by **Hayden Adams** with a $100K Ethereum Foundation grant, Uniswap revolutionizes DeFi and proves decentralized exchanges can actually work.

## How It Works

### **Automated Market Maker (AMM):**

**The Formula:** x × y = k

**Example:**

- Pool has 100 ETH and 50,000 DAI
- x × y = 100 × 50,000 = 5,000,000
- k must stay constant

**When You Swap:**

- You add ETH, remove DAI
- Ratio changes
- Price adjusts automatically
- No counterparty needed

### **Liquidity Providers:**

**The Innovation:**

- Users deposit token pairs (e.g., ETH + DAI)
- Become liquidity providers (LPs)
- Earn 0.3% fee on every trade
- Can withdraw anytime

**Why Revolutionary:**

- Passive income from trading fees
- No order management
- Automated yield
- Created "liquidity mining" concept

### **Impermanent Loss:**

**The Catch:**

- If prices diverge, LPs can lose money
- "Impermanent" because only realized when withdrawing
- Fees may or may not compensate
- Risk vs. reward calculation
