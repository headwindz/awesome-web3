## How The Hack Worked

**Ronin's Security Model:**

- **9 validator nodes** secure the bridge
- **5 of 9 signatures** needed to approve withdrawals
- Validators run by Sky Mavis (Axie devs) and allies

**What Hackers Did:**

1. **Compromised 4 Sky Mavis validators** through social engineering
2. **Compromised 1 Axie DAO validator** (December 2021 permission remained)

4. **Approved fraudulent withdrawals** to hacker addresses
5. **Drained bridge** over multiple transactions

**The Critical Vulnerability:**

In **November 2021**, Sky Mavis asked Axie DAO to help with load during high traffic. Axie DAO validator was given whitelist permission. This permission was **never revoked**.
