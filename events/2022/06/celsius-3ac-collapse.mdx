## The Celsius Death Spiral

**What Went Wrong:**

1. **GBTC Trade**:

   - Trapped billions in underwater position

2. **stETH Depeg**:

## The DeFi vs CeFi Debate

**Why DeFi Survived:**

- **Transparent**: On-chain, auditable
- **Liquidations worked**: Automatically
- **No counterparty risk**: Smart contracts
- **No fraud**: Code does what it says
- **Self-custody**: Users keep control

**Why CeFi Failed:**

- **Opaque**: Off-chain, trust-based
- **Hidden risks**: No one knew 3AC exposure
- **Counterparty risk**: Celsius, 3AC can lie
- **Fraud possible**: And it happened
- **Custodial**: Platform holds keys

**The Verdict:**

DeFi had **Black Thursday** (2020) but recovered.
CeFi had **Celsius/3AC** (2022) and many died.

Transparency > Promises.
