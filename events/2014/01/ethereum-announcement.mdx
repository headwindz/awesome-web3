title: Ethereum Announced - Smart Contracts Platform
tags: [ethereum, vitalik buterin, smart contracts, blockchain, announcement]
date: 2014-01-23
---

Vitalik Buterin publicly announces Ethereum at the North American Bitcoin Conference in Miami. Ethereum introduces the concept of a Turing-complete blockchain with smart contract functionality, going beyond Bitcoin's simple transactions to enable programmable decentralized applications.

The announcement laid the groundwork for DeFi, NFTs, DAOs, and thousands of decentralized applications that would follow, fundamentally expanding what blockchain technology could achieve.
