On November 28, 2012, at block height **210,000**, Bitcoin undergoes its **first halving event**—the mining reward drops from 50 BTC to 25 BTC per block. This moment establishes the **4-year cycle** that will come to define Bitcoin's price action and marks the beginning of Bitcoin's programmed scarcity playing out in real-time.

## The First Halving

### **What Happened:**

**Block 210,000:**

- Mining reward: 50 BTC → 25 BTC (50% reduction)
- First time in Bitcoin's history
- Exactly as Satoshi programmed
- Inflation rate: ~25% → ~12%
- **No bugs, no issues, worked perfectly**

**Bitcoin Price:**

- Day of halving: ~$12
- Had ranged $5-$13 most of 2012
- Relatively quiet event
- Most people didn't notice

### **The Mechanism:**

**Satoshi's Design:**

- 21 million Bitcoin maximum
- Halving every 210,000 blocks (~4 years)
- Predictable, immutable, programmatic
- No central bank, no discretion
- Mathematical certainty

**The Schedule:**

- 2009-2012: 50 BTC per block
- 2012-2016: 25 BTC per block
- 2016-2020: 12.5 BTC per block
- 2020-2024: 6.25 BTC per block
- 2024-2028: 3.125 BTC per block
- Continues until ~2140

## The Aftermath

### **2013: The Bull Run**

**What Followed:**

- January 2013: $13
- April 2013: $266 (20x in 4 months)
- Crash to $50
- November 2013: **$1,100** (Cyprus crisis, Chinese speculation)
- **100x from halving price**

**The Pattern Established:**

1. Halving occurs
2. 6-18 months later: Massive bull run
3. New all-time high
4. 80%+ crash
5. Bear market for 1-2 years
6. Repeat

### **Why It Mattered:**

**Proof of Concept:**

- Code executed perfectly
- No human intervention needed

- Scarcity narrative proven
- **Bitcoin actually works as programmed**

**Economic Impact:**

- Daily new supply cut from 7,200 BTC to 3,600 BTC

- Supply shock took time to materialize
- But when it hit, price exploded

## The Theory: Stock-to-Flow

### **Understanding Scarcity:**

**Stock-to-Flow Ratio:**

- Stock = Existing supply
- Flow = New production
- S2F = Stock ÷ Flow
- Higher S2F = More scarce

**Bitcoin's S2F Evolution:**

- Pre-halving: ~12 S2F
- Post-1st halving: ~25 S2F
- Post-2nd halving: ~50 S2F
- Post-3rd halving: ~100 S2F
- Post-4th halving: ~120 S2F

**Comparison:**

- Silver: S2F ~22
- Gold: S2F ~60
- Bitcoin (2024): S2F ~120
- **Bitcoin = Hardest money ever created**

### **The Supply Shock:**

**Simple Economics:**

- Demand stays constant or grows
- Supply cut in half
- Price must rise
- "Inevitable" according to believers

**Why Delay?**

- Takes time for supply shock to materialize
- Miners hold inventory
- Market efficiency isn't instant
- Psychology and momentum matter

## The Miners' Dilemma

### **Economic Reality:**

**Pre-Halving:**

**Post-Halving:**

- Mine 1 block = 25 BTC = $300
