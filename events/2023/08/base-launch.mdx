On August 9, 2023, **Coinbase** officially launched **Base**, its Ethereum Layer 2 network built on the OP Stack, marking the first time a **major publicly-traded company** launched a blockchain and signaling institutional embrace of decentralized infrastructure.

## What is Base?

**The Technical Details:**

- **Layer 2 solution** for Ethereum (OP Stack-based)
- **Built by Coinbase** (publicly traded, $30B+ market cap)
- **Optimistic Rollup** technology
- **1000x cheaper** gas fees than Ethereum mainnet
- **Fast transactions** (~2 seconds)
- **EVM equivalent** (easy for developers to port)

**The Vision:**

> "Bringing the next billion users onchain." - Brian Armstrong, Coinbase CEO

Base aimed to make crypto accessible to mainstream users through:

- Low transaction costs
- Coinbase brand trust
- Seamless fiat onramps
- Developer-friendly tooling
