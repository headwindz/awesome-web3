On March 23, 2023, **Do Kwon**, the fugitive founder of Terraform Labs behind the catastrophic Terra/Luna collapse, was **arrested at Podgorica airport in Montenegro** while attempting to board a flight to Dubai using a forged Costa Rican passport.

## The Hunt

**From Collapse to Manhunt:**

- **May 2022**: Terra/Luna implosion wipes out $40 billion
- **September 2022**: South Korean prosecutors issue arrest warrant
- **September 2022**: Interpol Red Notice issued
- **September-March 2023**: Do Kwon on the run

**Where Was He?**

- Claimed to be in Singapore (later proven false)
- Twitter activity suggested he was "not hiding"
- Traced to Serbia, then Montenegro
- Living under false identity

## The Arrest

**What Happened at Podgorica Airport:**

Montenegrin police discovered Do Kwon and **Han Chang-joon** (Terraform Labs CFO) attempting to board a private jet with:

- **Forged Costa Rican passports**
- **Belgian travel documents** (also fake)
- Destination: Dubai (no extradition treaty)

**The Charges in Montenegro:**

- Document forgery
- Initially sentenced to 4 months in prison
- Later extended as extradition proceedings began

## The International Tug-of-War

**Three Countries Wanted Him:**

1. **South Korea**:

   - Fraud charges
   - Violation of capital markets law
   - Wanted to prosecute at home

2. **United States**:

   - SEC charges: securities fraud
   - DOJ charges: fraud, conspiracy, market manipulation
   - Stronger case documentation

3. **Montenegro**:
   - Document forgery conviction
   - Must serve local sentence first

**The Extradition Battle:**

- Appeals dragged through 2023-2024
- Both US and South Korea filed competing requests
- Montenegro's courts had final say
- US eventually won extradition rights
