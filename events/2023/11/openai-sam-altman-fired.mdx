On November 17, 2023, **Sam Altman** is abruptly fired as CEO of OpenAI by the board, triggering a dramatic five-day crisis that ends with his return and a complete board overhaul. The chaos briefly impacts Worldcoin (WLD), Altman's crypto identity project.

## The Crisis

**Day 1 (Nov 17):** OpenAI board fires Altman citing loss of confidence

- CTO Mira Murati named interim CEO
- Co-founder Greg Brockman resigns in protest
- Tech world shocked

**Day 2-3:** Employee revolt

- 700+ of 770 employees threaten to quit
- Microsoft offers jobs to all OpenAI staff
- Pressure mounts on board

**Day 4:** Multiple CEO candidates cycle through

- Emmett Shear (Twitch co-founder) briefly named CEO
- Negotiations intensify

**Day 5 (Nov 22):** Altman returns triumphantly

- Nearly entire board replaced
- Governance structure reformed
- Microsoft gains board observer seat

## Crypto Connection

**Worldcoin Impact:**

Sam Altman's crypto project Worldcoin experiences volatility:

- **WLD token drops 15%** on firing news
- **Rebounds 20%** on return announcement
- Proves crypto markets react to Web2 tech drama

**What is Worldcoin?**

Altman's ambitious project to:

- Create global digital identity system
- Distribute UBI via biometric "Orb" scans
- Solve AI verification problem
- 2M+ users scanned by November 2023

## Why It Matters to Crypto

The saga highlights:

- Interconnection of AI and crypto ecosystems
- Governance challenges in mission-driven tech companies
- How Web2 leadership drama impacts Web3 projects
- Growing importance of AI-crypto convergence

Altman's dual role as OpenAI CEO and Worldcoin founder makes him a bridge between AI and crypto worlds, making this crisis relevant to both industries.
