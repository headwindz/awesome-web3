---
title: Trump Signs Executive Order on Digital Assets
tags: [trump, executive order, regulation, policy, bitcoin reserve]
date: 2025-01-23
---

On January 23, 2025, President Trump signed an executive order on digital assets that launched a federal working group, pushed agencies toward clearer rules, and directed exploration of a strategic Bitcoin reserve.

The order signaled a policy shift from enforcement heavy posture to innovation friendly coordination, and markets reacted with a sharp rally.

Why it mattered:

- It set a unified federal agenda for crypto policy.
- It opened the door to a Bitcoin reserve discussion.
- It boosted confidence in U.S. regulatory direction.
