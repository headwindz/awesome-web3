On August 28, 2020, an anonymous developer known as **Chef Nomi** launched **SushiSwap**, executing crypto's most audacious "**vampire attack**"‚Äîa hostile takeover that attempted to drain billions in liquidity from Uniswap by offering SUSHI token incentives to liquidity providers.

## The Setup: Uniswap's Vulnerability

**Uniswap's Situation in August 2020:**

- **Dominant DEX**: $500M+ in liquidity
- **No native token**: Just protocol fees (0.3%)
- **No loyalty mechanism**: LPs could leave anytime
- **Open source code**: Easily forkable
- **DeFi Summer peak**: Everyone seeking yield

**The Opportunity:**

Uniswap's liquidity providers were:

- Earning 0.3% trading fees only
- Not receiving any governance token
- Ripe for a better offer

## The Attack Vector

**How SushiSwap Worked:**

1. **Forked Uniswap's code** (open source)
2. **Added SUSHI token rewards** for liquidity providers
3. **Let users stake Uniswap LP tokens** on SushiSwap
4. **Promised migration**: After 2 weeks, migrate all liquidity to SushiSwap
5. **Developer rewards**: 10% of all SUSHI to developers

**The Incentive Structure:**

- Stake your **UNI-V2 LP tokens** on SushiSwap
- Earn **SUSHI tokens** (while still earning Uniswap fees)
- After 2 weeks: **Migration event** moves liquidity to SushiSwap
- Result: Same AMM, but now you have SUSHI tokens too

## The Migration Event

**September 9, 2020 - "The Great Migration":**

At block 10,850,000:

- **$1+ billion** in Uniswap liquidity staked on SushiSwap
- Migration function called
- Liquidity pulled from Uniswap ‚Üí moved to SushiSwap
- Uniswap TVL crashed ~70% overnight

**The Drama:**

This was **DeFi's first hostile takeover**‚Äîand it was working.

## Chef Nomi's Exit Scam (Almost)

**September 5, 2020 - 4 Days Before Migration:**

Chef Nomi, the anonymous founder:

- **Sold 2.5M SUSHI tokens** (~$13M)
- **Dumped on community** who trusted him
- **Twitter exploded** with rage
- **SUSHI price crashed** 50%+

**His Justification:**

> "I did nothing wrong. The SUSHI tokens were allocated to developers. I am just cashing out what I deserve." - Chef Nomi

**The Community Response:**

- Outrage and accusations of rug pull
- Calls to fork SushiSwap again
- Sam Bankman-Fried (FTX) and others stepped in
- Migration still happened (community commitment)

## The Redemption Arc

**September 11, 2020:**

Chef Nomi **returned all the funds**:

> "I fucked up. And I am sorry. I will return all the ETH back to the treasury." - Chef Nomi

**The Transfer:**

- Returned ~$14M to SushiSwap treasury
- Handed control to **Sam Bankman-Fried (FTX CEO)**
- Community governance activated
- Migration deemed successful

**SBF Takes Control:**

Sam Bankman-Fried became the interim leader and:

- Stabilized the protocol
- Implemented multi-sig
- Set up proper governance
- Eventually handed control to community (0xMaki and others)

## The Aftermath

**What Happened to Each Protocol:**

**Uniswap:**

- Lost $1B+ liquidity overnight
- **September 16, 2020**: Airdropped UNI token (response to SushiSwap)
- Recovered all liquidity within weeks
- Remained dominant DEX
- Launched V3 with innovations

**SushiSwap:**

- Successfully migrated liquidity
- Built sustainable community
- Expanded to multi-chain
- Added lending (Kashi), launchpad (MISO)
- Became top 5 DEX
- Still exists today (2026)

**Chef Nomi:**

- Disappeared after returning funds
- Never revealed identity
- Became cautionary tale
- Meme legend status

## The Innovation: Vampire Attacks

**What SushiSwap Invented:**

The **vampire attack** became a known DeFi strategy:

1. **Fork existing protocol**
2. **Add token incentives**
3. **Drain competitor's liquidity**
4. **Build on their work**

**Who Copied It:**

- **SpiritSwap** vampired SpookySwap (Fantom)
- **Tomb Finance** vampired other algorithmic stablecoins
- **UniSwap forks** across all chains
- Countless others tried and failed

## The Strategic Lessons

**What SushiSwap Proved:**

‚úÖ **Open source = vulnerable** to forks
‚úÖ **Liquidity is mercenary** without incentives
‚úÖ **Token rewards work** for bootstrapping
‚úÖ **Community can forgive** (if you return funds)
‚úÖ **First-mover advantage** isn't permanent

**What It Disproved:**

‚ùå **Forking guarantees success** (most forks died)
‚ùå **Anonymous founders are safe** (Chef Nomi nearly destroyed it)
‚ùå **Vampire attacks always work** (many failed)

## The Uniswap Response: UNI Token

**September 16, 2020 - One Week After SushiSwap Migration:**

Uniswap announced:

- **UNI governance token**
- **400 UNI airdrop** to every historical user
- **Worth $1,200+** at launch
- One of the largest airdrops ever

**The Irony:**

SushiSwap's vampire attack **forced Uniswap to launch a token**, which:

- Made early users wealthy
- Strengthened Uniswap's position
- Created governance alignment
- Became more valuable than SUSHI

**SushiSwap accidentally made Uniswap stronger.**
