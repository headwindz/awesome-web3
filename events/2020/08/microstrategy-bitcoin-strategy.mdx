On August 11, 2020, **MicroStrategy** announces it has purchased **21,454 BTC** for $250 million, adopting Bitcoin as its primary **treasury reserve asset**. CEO **Michael Saylor** becomes Bitcoin's most vocal corporate evangelist, setting off a wave of corporate adoption that changes Bitcoin from speculative asset to corporate treasury strategy.

## The Announcement

### **The Decision:**

**MicroStrategy's Thesis:**

- Cash is a "melting ice cube" (inflation eroding value)
- Fed's money printing = fiat currency debasement
- Need "digital gold" for treasury reserves
- Bitcoin = superior store of value

**The Purchase:**

- 21,454 BTC for $250 million
- Average price: ~$11,650
- Largest public company Bitcoin purchase ever
- Converted cash reserves to Bitcoin

**Michael Saylor's Conversion:**

**Before 2020:** Bitcoin skeptic

- Called Bitcoin "days are numbered" (2013)
- Dismissed as online gambling

**After COVID Stimulus:**

- Spent 1,000+ hours studying Bitcoin

- Became Bitcoin's loudest evangelist

## The Strategy Explained

### **Why Bitcoin for Treasury?**

**Traditional Options:**

- Cash: -7% to -15% real returns (inflation)
- Bonds: Negative real yields
- Stocks: Overvalued, volatile
- Real estate: Illiquid, expensive
- Gold: Hard to custody, verify, move

**Bitcoin's Advantages:**

- Fixed supply (21M cap)
- Digital (easy custody/transfer)
- Divisible (can sell small amounts)
- Liquid (24/7 global market)
- Transparent (on-chain verification)
- Portable (wallet, not warehouse)

### **The Leverage Play:**

**Capital Structure Arbitrage:**

1. Borrow cheap money (convertible debt at 0-1%)
2. Buy Bitcoin (expect >20% returns)
3. Bitcoin appreciates
4. Pay back debt
5. Shareholders win massively

**Or:**

1. Issue equity at premium to NAV
2. Use proceeds to buy Bitcoin
3. Accretive to BTC per share
4. Stock premium persists
5. Repeat (Bitcoin printer)

**The Risk:**

- If Bitcoin crashes, debt remains
- Margin calls possible
- Shareholder dilution
- Company becomes pure Bitcoin play

**Saylor's Bet:** Bitcoin will outperform everything. Full conviction.
