March 12-13, 2020: **"Black Thursday"** - Bitcoin crashes 50% in 24 hours, from $8,000 to **$3,800**, as COVID-19 pandemic triggers global financial panic. Ethereum falls to $90. MakerDAO nearly breaks. Liquidation cascades everywhere. But this crash marks the **beginning of crypto's greatest bull run**, fueled by unlimited money printing and a flight to decentralized assets.

## The Crash

### **Thursday, March 12, 2020:**

**Morning:** Bitcoin $7,900  
**Afternoon:** Panic selling begins  
**Evening:** $5,000 (down 36%)  
**Overnight:** Cascading liquidations  
**Friday morning:** **$3,800 low** (down 52% in 24 hours)

### **Ethereum Even Worse:**

- $190 → $90 (52% decline)
- Gas fees spike to 100+ gwei
- Network congestion
- MakerDAO liquidation crisis

### **Altcoins Annihilated:**

- Most down 60-80%
- XRP: $0.24 → $0.11
- Litecoin: $60 → $30
- Total crypto market cap: $235B → $110B (-53%)

## The Triggers

### **1. COVID-19 Pandemic:**

**The Timeline:**

- January: China lockdowns begin
- February: Spreads globally
- March 11: WHO declares pandemic
- March 12: Trump travel ban, markets panic
- March 13: U.S. national emergency

**Global Markets:**

- S&P 500: -10% (worst day since 1987)
- Dow: -2,997 points (10%)
- Oil: -25% in days
- Everything crashing together

### **2. "Digital Gold" Narrative Breaks:**

**The Theory:**

- Bitcoin = uncorrelated asset
- Safe haven during crisis
- Digital gold, inflation hedge
- Shouldn't crash with stocks

**The Reality:**

- Bitcoin fell HARDER than stocks
- Correlated during liquidity crisis
- "Risk asset" behavior
- Safe haven narrative shattered

**Why:**

- Traders needed cash (any cash)
- Margin calls forced liquidations
- Crypto most liquid to sell
- Flight to U.S. dollars

### **3. Leverage Liquidation Cascade:**

**The Death Spiral:**

1. Bitcoin drops 10%
2. Leveraged longs liquidated
3. More sell pressure
4. Price drops more
5. More liquidations
6. Repeat until capitulation

**BitMEX Overload:**

- Largest derivatives exchange
- System couldn't handle volume
- Trading halted multiple times
- Accusations of manipulation
- Liquidations at worst prices

### **4. DeFi Near-Death Experience:**

**MakerDAO Crisis:**

- Collateral value (ETH) crashed
- Liquidations triggered
- But network congestion prevented auctions
- "Zero bid" auctions occurred
- $0 paid for $8M+ in ETH collateral
- System nearly collapsed
- Emergency measures required

## The Response

### **Immediate:**

**Friday, March 13:**

- Bitcoin bounces to $5,300
- Still down 33% from start of week
- "Dead cat bounce" or bottom?

**Weekend:**

- Volatility continues
- $4,000-$6,000 range
- Uncertainty everywhere

### **Monday, March 16:**

**Stock Circuit Breakers:**

- S&P 500 drops 12% at open
- Trading halted
- Worst day since 1987 crash

**Bitcoin:**

- Remarkably stable at $5,000
- Decoupling narrative returns
- "Bitcoin doesn't have circuit breakers"
- 24/7 market = price discovery continues

**Before:** Digital gold (failed during crash)
